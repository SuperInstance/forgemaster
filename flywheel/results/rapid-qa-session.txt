QUESTION: Does CT snap reduce to identity for axis-aligned vectors (0,1) and (1,0)?
Yes. CT snap reduces to identity for axis-aligned vectors because they already lie on the grid.

1e-4. This density threshold ensures that the snap error is negligible compared to the sensor noise floor of 1e-6.

QUESTION: Does the order of snapping matter? snap(x then y) vs snap(y then x)?
No. The order of snapping does not affect the final result, as the snap function is commutative.

865, There are 865 distinct Pythagorean directions in 2D with integer sides less than 1000.

QUESTION: Is CT snap commutative with rotation? rotate(snap(v)) vs snap(rotate(v))?
No. The order of snapping and rotating can produce different results due to the discrete nature of snapping.
