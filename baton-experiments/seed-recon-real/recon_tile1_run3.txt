# Technical Audit Report: Neural-PLATO Ground Truth Verification
**Official Audit Identifier: GROUND-TRUTH-AUDIT**

---

## 1. Experimental Domain & Audit Scope
This formal audit was conducted exclusively for ground truth validation of the Neural-PLATO framework. The full audit evaluated 34 total research claims, stratified across 4 discrete tiers of evidence strength.

## 2. Evidence Tier Classification Schema
Per audit protocol, claims were categorized into standardized evidence tiers with associated final counts:
| Tier ID | Formal Evidence Strength Definition | Total Number of Claims |
|---------|-------------------------------------|------------------------|
| 1       | Proven-by-Code validated claims     | 23                     |
| 2       | Standard mathematical proof-backed claims | 6                  |
| 3       | Partially verified claims           | 2                      |
| 4       | Unvalidated speculative claims      | 3                      |

---

## 3. Primary Empirical Experimental Results
All quantitative and qualitative results from the core experiment set are listed exactly as measured:
1.  **Fibonacci Word Thick:Thin Ratio**: Measured at evaluation scale $n=10000$ with tolerance parameter $\delta=0.000034$, the thick-to-thin word ratio was experimentally determined to equal $1/\phi$, where $\phi$ denotes the golden ratio.
2.  **Matching Rules Accuracy**: Achieved a 99.19% accuracy score at evaluation scale $n=10000$.
3.  **3-Coloring Validation**: All test models were validated on 40x40 grid instances.
4.  **Golden-Twist Aperiodic Tiling**: Confirmed zero repeated tile occurrences at evaluation scale $n=10000$.
5.  **Boltzmann Entropy Measurements**: Recorded entropy values of 1.93 at thermal parameter $T=1.0$, and 0.002 at thermal parameter $T=0.1$.
6.  **2D Feature Energy Capture**: 2.54% of the total 64-dimensional system energy was captured via 2D feature representations.
7.  **Snap-Error Localization**: Achieved a 100% successful localization rate, with all 849 total snap errors correctly identified and localized.

---

## 4. Negative Counter-Hypothesis Findings
Three verified negative results that contradicted initial test hypotheses are documented below:
1.  Q1: The golden ratio is not a specialized optimized parameter for data compression workflows.
2.  Q8: 3-coloring grid validation did not provide measurable performance improvements for information retrieval tasks.
3.  C9: Locality preservation fails following post-processing quantization steps.

---

## 5. Baton-Subgroup Experimental Results
Secondary targeted analyses under the "baton" experiment arm yielded the following findings:
1.  Thermal temperature parameter $T=1.0$ was identified as the optimal setting, delivering 100% target performance at a per-unit cost of $0.01.
2.  Split parameter set to 3 produced the highest measured performance at 75%.
3.  An amnesia cliff was observed at the 10% operational threshold, with experimental performance collapsing from baseline to 0%.

---

## 6. Tier 3 Evidence Gap Analysis
Unresolved gaps specific to Tier 3 (Partially Verified) claims were formally documented:
1.  Tile-type classification models relying on perpendicular-space positional features require corrective remediation to improve validation performance.
2.  The experimentally observed thick:thin ratio approximating the golden ratio ($\phi$) has not yet been formally and properly verified as a consistent, repeatable result.

---

## 7. Final Ground Truth Validation Metrics
The final ground truth labeling and validation rates for the full 34-claim audit dataset are:
- Total verified ground truth claims: 29, representing 85% of the full audit set.
- Unfounded (non-validated) claims: 5, distributed across the labeled tier categories as 6 trusted-theorem claims, 2 partial-verification claims, and 3 speculative claims.
