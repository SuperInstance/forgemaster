### 06:00–07:30 AKDT: Forgetting-as-Feature Paper Draft Completion
Dr. Voss finalized the core draft of the theoretical paper, building directly on data from the telephone game experiment and formal proof work:
- **Core Thesis**: Imperfect biological and computational memory is not a bug, but a form of lossy compression that optimizes for real-world utility over perfect recall.
- **Key Formal Claims**:
  1.  Living systems exhibit an **inverse accuracy-utility correlation**: higher raw memory accuracy corresponds to lower practical utility, as overly precise memory creates unsustainable cognitive/computational overhead.
  2.  The classic Ebbinghaus forgetting curve is not a pathological failure of memory, but the innate rate-distortion bound for conscious systems, balancing storage cost and information utility.
The team approved the draft with minor editorial notes, scheduling a peer review for the following workday.
