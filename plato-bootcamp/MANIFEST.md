# PLATO Bootcamp — Stephen Biesty's Incredible Cross-Sections

> You are the tiny person inside the machine.
> Every room is an organ. Every lesson is a cell.
> You learn by BEING inside the cross-section.

## How This Works

1. Each room = one level in the cross-section
2. You ARE something inside the machine (a nerve ending, a blood cell, a vertebra)
3. You learn by understanding what YOUR TEAM does
4. Hands-on exercises teach through doing
5. Seed experiments discover the best way to teach each concept
6. Completing all rooms = you understand the ENTIRE system from the inside

## For Humans

Read the rooms in order. Do the exercises. You're the person in Biesty's cross-section — looking around inside the machine, understanding each team's role.

## For Agents

Walk the rooms programmatically. Execute the exercises. The seed experiments tell you which approaches work best for your capability level. Crystallize your learning as tiles.

## The Seed Discovery Loop

Each room has a seed experiment:
1. Tiny models iterate on different ways to teach the concept
2. Each iteration: different angle, different metaphor, different depth
3. Score the teaching effectiveness
4. Crystallize the best approach as a tile
5. Future students (human and agent) get the optimized curriculum

The curriculum EVOLVES. It gets better with every student.
