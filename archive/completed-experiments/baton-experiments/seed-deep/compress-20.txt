### Full Reconstructed TTRPG Session: *Forgemaster CTE Complete*
*System: Dungeons & Dragons 5e, Party Level 7*
This reconstruction expands on the compressed shorthand note, where "CTE" was party-shorthand for **Combat Tactical Engagement** (the formal name for the large-scale boss fight):

---

## Session Lead-In
The party wrapped up a guild delivery contract in Bramble’s End, a frontier town, when the local blacksmiths’ union hired them to shut down a rogue forging operation run by disgraced former guild master Thrain Ironvein—dubbed the "Forgemaster" by local laborers. Thrain had abducted travelers and miners to melt down their bone and living flesh to forge cursed, sentient weapons in a repurposed mountain blast furnace refinery on the edge of the Frostspine Hills.

The party traveled to the refinery, fought off a pack of starved winter wolves drawn to the forge’s constant heat, and freed two bound captive miners who shared intel on Thrain’s defenses: two iron golem enforcers patrolling the foundry floor, and a permanent curtain of molten slag that blocked all long-range attacks within 30 feet of his central anvil workstations.

---

## The Forgemaster CTE (Combat Tactical Engagement)
The party breached the refinery’s outer gates using smoke bombs to mask their approach, then disabled the slag vent to shut down the protective fire curtain mid-fight. Thrain heard their intrusion, slammed his fist on his anvil, and summoned his iron golem guards as he stepped forward wielding a massive starsteel warhammer. The fight unfolded as:
1.  **Opening Volley**: The bard cast *Faerie Fire* to expose the golems’ weak joints, the rogue tossed a net to pin one golem’s movement, the wizard’s opening Fire Bolt fizzled against the golems’ slag-coated armor, and the paladin charged to melee the pinned golem.
2.  **Forgemaster’s First Strike**: Thrain slammed his starsteel hammer into the forge floor, sending a wave of superheated molten slag across the northern foundry, dealing fire damage to three party members and knocking the rogue prone.
3.  **Mid-Fight Turnaround**: The cleric used *Lay on Hands* to heal the rogue and buff the paladin with *Divine Favor*. The wizard swapped to *Thunderwave*, which shattered the pinned golem’s slag coating and exposed its internal core for bonus damage. The paladin landed a critical hit that destroyed the exposed golem outright. The remaining golem grappled the wizard, but the rogue used thieves’ tools to pop its joint locks mid-turn.
4.  **Finishing Push**: Thrain retreated to the central anvil and activated a hidden fire core beneath it, wrapping himself in a swirling barrier of superheated flame. The party countered with a pre-stashed Potion of Fire Resistance drunk by the paladin, the bard casting *Shield of the Hidden Lord* to grant frontliners fire immunity, and the rogue tossed an alchemical frost vial to cool Thrain’s flame barrier. The paladin landed a finishing Divine Smite critical hit, shattering Thrain’s starsteel hammer and reducing him to a pile of molten slag and broken forging tools.

---

## Post-CTE Wrap-Up
After the combat concluded, the party:
1.  Looted Thrain’s workshop: recovered his cracked starsteel hammer shard, two Potions of Fire Resistance, three cursed sentient iron daggers, and blueprints for his flesh-forged cursed weapons.
2.  Freed the 7 remaining captive miners and travelers hiding in the refinery’s basement, who helped them pack leftover forging materials to turn over to the guild for a 500gp total bounty.
3.  Had their outstanding custom armor commission erased by the blacksmiths’ union after turning over the cursed daggers, and earned a permanent 25% discount at all guild-affiliated blacksmith shops across the Sword Coast, plus class-tailored enchanted weapons as a bonus reward.

The session closed with the party resting at the refinery before traveling back to Bramble’s End to collect their final payment.
