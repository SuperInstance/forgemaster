# Neural-PLATO Ground Truth Verification Audit Experiment
## Audit Official Identifier: GROUND-TRUTH-AUDIT

---

## 1. Document Purpose & Experimental Domain
This technical document catalogs all verified data, raw metrics, negative findings, unresolved gaps, and ground truth validation results from the ground truth verification audit campaign for the Neural-PLATO AI research platform. All content is pulled directly from official audit records with no supplementary fabricated information added.

## 2. Experimental Claim Pool & Evidence Tier Taxonomy
The audit evaluated 34 total claims, grouped into 4 hierarchical tiers based on the rigor of supporting evidence required for validation:
| Tier Label | Evidence Strength Definition | Number of Claims | Percent of Total Pool |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | Proven via executable, reproducible code | 23 | 67.6% |
| Tier 2 | Validated via widely accepted standard mathematical theorems | 6 | 17.6% |
| Tier 3 | Partially verified with incomplete supporting evidence | 2 | 5.9% |
| Tier 4 | Unvalidated exploratory speculative claims | 3 | 8.8% |
*Total audited claims: 23 + 6 + 2 + 3 = 34 (matches official audit pool size)*

## 3. Primary Quantitative Experimental Results
All metrics below use a fixed sample size of *n=10000* unless explicitly noted, with statistical uncertainty tolerance Δ=0.000034 applied to proportional measurements:
1.  **Fibonacci Word Thick:Thin Ratio**: Measured at 1/φ (the inverse golden ratio), matching predicted theoretical tiling values
2.  **Matching Rules Compliance**: 99.19% of tested samples adhered to framework-defined matching rules
3.  **40x40 3-Coloring Verification**: Successful formal validation of a 40x40 grid 3-coloring problem via the audit platform
4.  **Golden-Twist Aperiodic Tile Set**: Demonstrated 0 repeated tile configurations across 10000 full test iterations, confirming aperiodicity
5.  **Boltzmann Entropy Measurements**:
    - At thermal temperature T=1.0: Recorded entropy value = 1.93
    - At thermal temperature T=0.1: Recorded entropy value = 0.002
6.  **Cross-Dimensional Energy Capture**: 2-dimensional projections captured 2.54% of total measured energy from the full 64-dimensional input space
7.  **Snap-Error Localization**: 100% accurate detection and localization of all reported snap errors, with 849 out of 849 total snap errors correctly identified.

## 4. Negative Non-Confirmatory Findings
Audit identified three claims where hypothesized performance gains or theoretical properties failed to be corroborated, tagged with their original audit claim identifiers:
1.  **Q1: Golden Ratio Compression Specialness**: Empirical data disproves that the golden ratio is a specialized optimal construct for general data compression tasks
2.  **Q8: 3-Coloring as Retrieval Aid**: Deployment of 3-coloring problem frameworks did not provide measurable performance improvements for information retrieval workflows
3.  **C9: Post-Quantization Locality**: The hypothesized retention of spatial locality after neural network quantization failed experimentally; no such locality effect was observed.

## 5. Baton-Platform Secondary Experimental Results
Sub-audit experiments run on the Baton evaluation framework yielded the following performance and behavior metrics:
1.  **Optimal Thermal Temperature**: T=1.0 was identified as the optimal temperature setting, delivering 100% target task performance at a per-run operational cost of $0.01
2.  **Optimal Split Configuration**: A data split value of 3 was determined to be the optimal partitioning strategy, delivering 75% performance on the target task
3.  **Amnesia Cliff Phenomenon**: A sharp, abrupt performance collapse (termed an "amnesia cliff") was observed when model training data retention was reduced to 10% of the full dataset size, with performance dropping immediately to 0%.

## 6. Unresolved Tier 3 Verification Gaps
Two critical unresolved gaps were identified for the partially verified (Tier 3) claim pool:
1.  **Tile-Type Classification Flaw**: Automated tile-type classification based on perpendicular-space position is an unreliable method that requires corrective updates or full re-validation
2.  **Unverified Thick:Thin Ratio**: The experimentally measured approximate phi-based thick:thin ratio has not yet been formally and properly verified to confirm alignment with golden ratio theoretical predictions.

## 7. Final Ground Truth Validation Metrics
Audit calculated the overall ground truth concordance rate for the 34 claims:
- Validated ground-truth confirmed claims: 29, representing 85% of the total experimental pool
- Unfounded (non-validated) claims: 5, with a labeled breakdown across audit tags: 6 trusted-theorem labeled claims, 2 partially verified claims, and 3 speculative claims (per official audit documentation).
