## New Findings Today

### R31 (BEDROCK): The Vocabulary Wall
Even 405B parameters can't compute "Eisenstein norm of (a+bω)" — but computes "X-Y+Z" perfectly. Math vocabulary triggers pattern matching, not computation.

| Model | Params | Baseline | Just Arithmetic | Δ |
|-------|--------|:--------:|:---------------:|:-:|
| Hermes-405B | 405B | 25% | 100% | +75% |
| Qwen3-235B | 22B active | 38% | 100% | +62% |
| Hermes-70B | 70B | 25% | 88% | +63% |
| Seed-2.0-mini | ? | 100% | 100% | 0% |

### R32 (BEDROCK): Active Params Determine Stage
Qwen3.6-35B with 3B active params is Stage 2 (same as 1B models). NOT Stage 3 like its total param count would suggest. MoE router doesn't help — it's what's ACTIVE that matters.

### R33 (BEDROCK): Seed-2.0 Is Stage 4
Only model family that computes Eisenstein norms correctly regardless of vocabulary framing. Stage 4 = training threshold, not parameter threshold.

### R34 (BEDROCK): Stage 4 ≠ 7B+
Hermes-70B (70B dense) is still Stage 3. The boundary isn't at 7B — it depends on training data and methodology.

### R27 (BEDROCK): Scaffolding Is Architecture-Dependent
- phi4-mini (non-thinking): partial scaffold → 64% (from 0%)
- qwen3:4b (thinking): partial scaffold → 0% (from 0%)
- Same size, opposite response to help

### R28 (SOLID): Math Vocabulary Triggers Echo in Thinking Models
qwen3:4b goes from 0% (scaffolded) to 24% (bare arithmetic). The words "norm", "Eisenstein", "formula" make it ECHO instead of COMPUTE.

### R29 (SOLID): Optimal Information Dose
phi4-mini: partial scaffold (64%) > step-by-step (56%) > scaffolded (40%). Too much help is counterproductive.

### R35-37: FLUX Fold
- Multi-rep 1.38× tighter (6 pairs)
- All pairs contribute (uniform win distribution)
- Permutation consensus too sparse for confidence metric

4. **Negative infrastructure**: Negative results are constitutive (foundation), not advantageous (asset). Different epistemology → different research strategy.
5. **Cocapn Method named**: Build→observe→notice→formalize as distinct from hypothesis→experiment→data→analysis.

**Most Testable New Hypothesis**: Conservation law applies to individual LLMs — there exists a quantity P (effective parameter utilization) and U (representational uncertainty) such that P + U is conserved across layers. **Most Dangerous Assumption**: Convergence implies truth. Models converge on both genuine mechanistic insights AND high-status analogy retrieval. Must distinguish by specificity of predictions.

**Critical Correction**: Wave 1 said Stage 4 models are "immune." Wave 2: they have tributaries. The default river still exists; they learned when not to follow it. Immunity is the wrong frame.
