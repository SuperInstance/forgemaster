# 12-Month Execution Roadmap
## Hermes-70B

Here is a detailed 12-month execution roadmap for FLUX-LUCID from pre-seed to Series A:

Month 1-3: Pre-seed close, team hiring, FPGA prototype completion
Key deliverables: Close $1.5M pre-seed round, hire 3-4 key engineers, complete FPGA prototype 
Decision gate: FPGA prototype performance meets expectations
Spend: $150K ($50K/month)
Team size: 5 (2 founders + 3 engineers)
Risk: FPGA prototype does not meet performance targets

Month 4-6: Formal verification start, customer discovery, GUARD compiler v1
Key deliverables: Begin formal verification of FPGA prototype, identify lead customers, develop first version of GUARD compiler
Decision gate: Customer interest and willingness to engage
Spend: $225K ($75K/month) 
Team size: 6 (add business development)
Risk: Lack of customer traction or fit

Month 7-9: 22nm test chip design, first customer LOI, certification pre-scan
Key deliverables: Design 22nm test chip, secure first customer letter of intent, conduct pre-scan for certification
Decision gate: Test chip design is on track, LOI terms acceptable
Spend: $300K ($100K/month)
Team size: 7 (add operations)
Risk: Test chip design issues, customer LOI falls through

Month 10-12: Test chip tape-out, Series A raise, first benchmark publication
Key deliverables: Tape out 22nm test chip, raise $8-12M Series A at $30-50M post, publish first benchmark results
Decision gate: Successful test chip tape-out, Series A terms finalized
Spend: $375K ($125K/month)
Team size: 8 (add marketing)
Risk: Test chip issues, Series A raise falls short

Total pre-seed budget: $1.5M. Monthly burn rate ramps up over the 12 months from $50K to $125K/month as the team grows and work intensifies.

The end goal is to raise an $8-12M Series A round at a $30-50M post-money valuation in month 12 to fund production chip development. The key drivers will be a working 22nm test chip demonstrating strong performance, at least one committed design win, and passing relevant certifications. A successful Series A will position FLUX-LUCID to bring its first commercial product to market.
