KEY FINDINGS (in priority order):

1. ARCHITECTURE > PRECISION for conservation.
   - Random coupling: CV=0.032 (conserved) regardless of precision
   - Hebbian/Attention: CV=0.12-0.15 (NOT conserved) regardless of precision
   - This is the strongest finding. Conservation is an eigenvalue-statistics property.

2. ASYMMETRIC COUPLING PRESERVES CONSERVATION.
   - Direction-dependent precision loss does NOT break the law
   - Paradoxically improves conservation (lower CV than symmetric)
   - FP64/INT4 asymmetric: CV=0.0000 (frozen conservation)

3. C IS FLAT ACROSS PRECISION (5% variation from 2-bit to 64-bit).
   - Confirms GLM-5.1's substrate-invariant claim with cleaner data.

4. TERNARY is the precision floor for heterogeneous fleets.
   - FP32+ternary: survives (CV=0.13)
   - FP32+binary: breaks conservation (CV=0.46)

5. BBP BROADENING: inconclusive. Block matrix methodology too artificial.
