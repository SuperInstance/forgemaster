Cycle 5 (Nemotron-30B, rotation 2) — Nonlinear Dynamics & Temperature

KEY RESULTS:
1. TEMPERATURE PREDICTION CONFIRMED: CV(γ+H) monotonically decreases with τ (0.057→0.0002). Softmax brief validated.
2. Pure noise BREAKS conservation (CV=0.194, TrC² CV=1.374). First genuine falsification.
3. Two-moment theory PARTIALLY WRONG: R²=0.20 for attention (Tr(C)+Tr(C²) don't explain γ+H variation).
4. Row-stochasticity pins Tr(C²) EXACTLY (CV=0 for all τ). Eigenvalue component of theory is correct.
5. Residual γ+H variation comes from eigenvector rotation, not captured by trace moments.
6. Nonlinear dynamics (tanh + state-dependent coupling) are essential — power iteration and fixed coupling give trivial results.
7. Normalized Hebbian has strong γ-H anti-correlation (-0.685) but still CV=0.053.

THEORY REVISION:
- Two-moment theory is necessary but not sufficient
- Need eigenvector dynamics theory to explain residual variation
- Conservation quality = eigenvalue stability (solved) + eigenvector stability (open problem)
