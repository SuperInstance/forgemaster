Safety Certification Costs $50–100M

DO-178C certification for a single flight control system takes years and millions. Most of that cost is proving your runtime checks are correct. FLUX eliminates the proof burden by compiling correct-by-construction constraints.

GUARD DSL

Write constraints in a domain-specific language designed for safety engineers. Envelope limits, invariants, and tolerances — no Rust expertise needed.

Formal Proofs

Every constraint is machine-proven correct. SMT solvers discharge proof obligations at compile time. No unverified runtime assertions.

Hardware Speed

Compiled constraints run at over 1 billion checks/sec on COTS hardware. Zero allocations, zero branching overhead. Proven fast.
